I'm a Computer Science student and creative technologist who enjoys combining design, technology, and visual storytelling. My experience spans web design, graphic design, data operations, and professional drone work, giving me a well-rounded approach to building and presenting digital content.

I'm always learning and expanding my skills while building a portfolio of projects that showcase both technical ability and creativity.
